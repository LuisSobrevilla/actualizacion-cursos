Listen and repeat

Instrucciones: En esta sección escucharás nuevamente el diálogo, pero ahora trata de repetirlo. Hazlo cuantas veces lo necesites hasta que sientas que comprendes lo que estás escuchando y que dominas la pronunciación de cada palabra.


Diálogo A: Conociendo gente en la escuela

  • Teacher: Hello.
  • Carlos: Hola! Hello
  • Teacher: My name is Ms. Rosario. I’m your new teacher. It’s nice to meet you. What is your first name?
  • Carlos: Carlos. My name is Carlos. It’s nice to meet you too.
  • Teacher: Class, this is Carlos. He is new to our school.
  • Anthony: Hi, Carlos. I’ am Anthony. It’s nice to meet you, Carlos. Where are you from?
  • Carlos: Hello Anthony. I’m from Guadalajara, Mexico.
  • Anthony: Are you here with your family?
  • Carlos: Yes, I’m here with my mother, my father, my two sisters and my brother.

Diálogo B: Conociendo gente en la oficina

  • Mr. Anderson: Hello, are you Mr. Jimenez?
  • Mr. Jimenez: Yes. I am Eduardo Jimenez.
  • Mr. Anderson: Mr. Jimenez, my name is Jim Anderson. I’m the supervisor. Nice to meet you
  • Mr. Jimenez: Nice to meet you too, Mr. Anderson.
  • Mr. Anderson: Mr. Jimenez, this is Ann Jones the secretary.
  • Anne: Nice to meet you Mr. Jimenez.
  • Mr Jimenez: Nice to meet you too.
  • Mr. Anderson: If you need anything, ask me or Mrs. Jones.
  • Mr. Jimenez: Thank you very much.
  • Mr. Anderson: You are welcome. Let’s get to work

Diálogo C: Conociendo gente en una fiesta

  • Angela: Oh, hello, John. It’s so nice to see you again. How are you?
  • John: I’m fine, thanks. Happy birthday, Angela. It’s nice to see you too. How are you?
  • Angela: I’m fine. Thanks for coming to the party.
  • John: Angela, this is my friend, Laura.
  • Angela: Hi, Laura.
  • Laura: Hello, Angela. Happy Birthday.
  • Angela: Thank you. It’s nice to meet you, Laura.
  • Laura: Here is a present for you.
  • Angela: Oh, thank you. That’s very nice of you. Would you like something to drink?
  • Laura: Yes. A lemonade please.
  • Angela: John, would you like something to drink?
  • John: A Coke please.
  • Angela: Here you are. Enjoy!
  • Laura, John: Thanks!
  • Angela: You’re welcome.
